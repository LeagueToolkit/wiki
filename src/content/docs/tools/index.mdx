## Everything else

The LeagueToolkit organization also maintains
[awesome-league](https://github.com/LeagueToolkit/awesome-league), a curated
list of League modding tools and resources. It covers:

- Community projects outside the LeagueToolkit organization
- Older tools from the C# era, including the original
  [LeagueToolkit C# library](https://github.com/LeagueToolkit/LeagueToolkit),
  Fantome, and LeagueFileTranslator

If you landed here from an old tutorial referencing a tool not listed above,
awesome-league is the place to check its status.

cslol-manager is deprecated in favor of LTK Manager. See
[Coming from cslol-manager](/start-here/from-cslol/).
:::
