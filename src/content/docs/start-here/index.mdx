If you have never modded League of Legends before, work through these pages in
order. It takes about fifteen minutes end to end, and most of that is waiting
for downloads.

## The short version

<Steps>

1. **Understand what you're installing.** A mod does not edit your game files -
   it builds a separate overlay that League reads instead.
   [How Modding Works](/start-here/how-modding-works/)

2. **Install LTK Manager.** One desktop app handles downloading, organizing, and
   applying mods. [Install LTK Manager](/start-here/install/)

3. **Get a mod.** Mods are shared as `.modpkg` or `.fantome` files on community
   sites and Discord servers. [Where to Find Mods](/start-here/finding-mods/)

4. **Apply it and play.** Enable the mod, hit **Patch**, launch League.
   [Your First Mod](/start-here/first-mod/)

</Steps>
