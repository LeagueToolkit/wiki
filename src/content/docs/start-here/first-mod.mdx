This guide walks you through installing and applying your first mod with ltk-manager.

## Prerequisites

- ltk-manager installed ([Installation guide](/start-here/install/))
- League of Legends installed and up to date
- A mod file in `.modpkg` or `.fantome` format

## Finding a Mod

Community mod sites and Discord servers are common sources for League mods. Mods are distributed as `.fantome` or `.modpkg` files, and LTK Manager installs both - you do not need to care which one you downloaded. See [Where to Find Mods](/start-here/finding-mods/).

## Installing the Mod

There are two ways to install a mod:

### Drag and Drop

Drag a `.modpkg` or `.fantome` file directly into the LTK Manager window. You can drop multiple files at once for bulk installation.

### File Browser

1. Click the **Install** button in the library toolbar
2. Select one or more mod files
3. Confirm the installation

Installed mods appear in your library and are automatically enabled.

## Applying Mods

Once you have mods installed and enabled:

1. Click the **Patch** button in the status bar
2. Wait for the overlay to build (this combines your enabled mods)
3. The patcher will apply the overlay to League
4. Launch League of Legends - your mods are active

To remove mods from the game, click **Stop** on the patcher.

## Verifying the Mod

Launch a game and check that your mod's changes are visible in-game. If mods aren't showing:

- Confirm the mods are **enabled** in your library (check the toggle)
- Make sure the patcher is running (click **Patch** in the status bar)
- Check that you started the patcher **after** enabling the mod
