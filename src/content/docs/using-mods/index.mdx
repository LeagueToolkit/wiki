## The two rules worth memorising

Almost every "my mods stopped working" report comes down to one of these:

1. **Enable first, patch second.** The patcher builds its overlay from whatever
   is enabled at the moment you press **Patch**. Enabling a mod afterwards does
   nothing until you stop and re-patch.
2. **A game patch invalidates your overlay.** After League updates, mods need to
   be re-applied and some need updating by their author. See
   [Updating After a Patch](/using-mods/patch-updates/).
